Lasanga

Description

Lasanga originated in italy during the Middle AgesThe oldest know written reference to lasanga apperas in 1282 in a ballad transcribed by a Bolognese notary, "Pur bii del vin, comadre, e no lo temperare" ('Just drink some wine, my woman, and do not dilute it '), part of the Memoriali Bolognesi (lit. 'Bolognese Memorials').

Ingredients

  • For the meat Sauce
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 onion (chopped)
    • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
    • 500 g ground beef or minced meat
    • 2 cups tomato sauce
    • 2 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 tsp basil
    • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • For the Cheese Layer
    • 2 cups ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
    • 2 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
    • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese (grated)
    • 1 egg
  • Other Ingredients
    • 12 lasagna sheets/noodles
    • Water for boiling noodles
    • Butter or oil for greasing the baking dish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
    2. Add chopped onions and cook until soft.
    3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    4. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
    5. Pour in tomato sauce and tomato paste.
    6. Add salt, pepper, oregano, basil, and chili flakes.
    7. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
    1. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and parmesan cheese.
    2. Mix well.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Sheets
    1. Boil water in a large pot.
    2. Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
    3. Drain and keep aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    1. Grease a baking dish.
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
    3. Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
    4. Spread cheese mixture.
    5. Add mozzarella cheese.
    6. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used.
    7. Finish with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake
    1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden.
  6. Serve
    1. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes.
    2. Slice and serve hot.
