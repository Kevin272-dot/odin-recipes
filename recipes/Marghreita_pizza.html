Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita Pizza is a classic Italian pizza known for its simple yet delicious combination of fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves on a crispy crust. Its light flavors and cheesy texture make it one of the most loved pizza varieties around the world.

Ingredients

  • For the Dough
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • For the Topping
    • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
    • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
    • Tomato slices
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil

    Steps

    1. Prepare the Dough
      1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast.
      2. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
      3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil.
      4. Knead into a soft dough.
      5. Let it rise for 1 hour.
    2. Shape the Pizza
      1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
      2. Roll the dough into a circle.
      3. Place on a baking tray.
    3. Add Toppings
      1. Spread pizza sauce.
      2. Add mozzarella cheese.
      3. Place tomato slices and basil leaves.
      4. Drizzle olive oil.
    4. Bake
      1. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
      2. Remove when crust is golden.
    5. Serve
      1. Slice the pizza.
      2. Serve hot.
